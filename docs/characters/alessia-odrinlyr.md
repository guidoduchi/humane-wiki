# Alessia Odrinlyr

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Alessia Odrinlyr is one of the main crew and the daughter of Odrin.

## Identity

- Kelta / Bell Beaker-descended girl.
- Formerly named Ayka in older drafts.
- Hyper Ability: Hyper Fabric.

## Narrative Function

Alessia is a problem-breaker and social tactician.

Her intelligence creates solutions, but also creates the danger of control, manipulation, and emotional distance.
