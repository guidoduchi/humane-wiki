# Wyllford / Wyll

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Wyllford, usually called Wyll, is Erlen's closest friend.

## Identity

- Pritani boy.
- Calm, dry, loyal, observant, and quietly burdened.
- Hyper Ability: Hyper Sense.

## Narrative Function

Wyll is a sensory and moral anchor.

His perception makes him useful, but it also means the world hurts him more sharply than others realize.
