# Erlen Dhalmurr

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Erlen Dhalmurr is the opening protagonist of HUMANE.

## Identity

- Pritani boy.
- Son of Senara Dhalmurr.
- Grandson of Maedra Dhalmurr.
- Hyper Ability: Hyper Focus.

## Narrative Function

Erlen carries the central pressure of impossible power meeting stubborn humanity.

His story should test whether kindness can survive fear, hunger, grief, and cosmic scale.
