# Muyu Rumi

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Muyu Rumi is the outsider whose arrival proves the Star Piece connects worlds beyond Britain.

## Identity

- Machalilla / Galapagos-linked girl.
- Rebellious, instinctive, playful, mysterious, homesick, and fiercely independent.
- Hyper Ability: Hyper Instinct.

## Narrative Function

Muyu enters through rescue and displacement rather than exposition.

Her arc carries survival instinct, longing for home, and the cost of saving someone while losing your own place in the world.
