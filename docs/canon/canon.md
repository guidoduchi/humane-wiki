## Core Identity

**HUMANE** is a Bronze Age science-fantasy story set mainly in the British Isles around **1159 B.C.E.**, during the wider Bronze Age collapse.

The story begins in a grounded human world of food, ritual, weather, trade pressure, children, tired adults, and political stress. Then a tiny engineered object — the Star Piece — falls into that world and turns human choices into cosmic consequences.

## Core Question

> What makes a human humane when cruelty becomes logical?

## Current Opening Lock

The opening movement begins in Pritana, at Vahllalhag, shortly before a journey toward Mel Tor and Sharrah Pool.

The Star Piece should enter through wonder, fear, and confusion — not exposition.
