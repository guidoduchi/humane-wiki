# Hyper Abilities

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Hyper Abilities are not free superpowers.

They are human traits forced into extreme, costly forms by Star Piece interaction or influence.

## Working Rule

Every Hyper Ability should have:

- a visible strength,
- a biological or psychological cost,
- a narrative temptation,
- a vulnerability,
- an emotional consequence.
