# Star Piece

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

The Star Piece is a tiny engineered object that enters the Bronze Age world as something people can only interpret through myth, omen, fear, and wonder.

## Visual Canon

- Around 2 cm wide.
- Icosahedral geometry.
- Thick golden outer structure.
- Dark ebony triangular faces.
- Some translucent facets.
- Interior stellated geometry.
- Golden singularity-like center.

## Narrative Rule

The Star Piece should create problems more often than it solves them.

It expands the scale of the story, but it should never erase the human cost of hunger, grief, fear, love, and choice.
