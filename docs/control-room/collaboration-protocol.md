# Collaboration Protocol

Status: Working Board
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

This page defines how humans, editors, developers, and AI assistants should interact with the HUMANE wiki.

## Shared Goal

Help develop **HUMANE** into a stronger, clearer, more emotionally powerful novel without flattening its mystery or over-explaining its world.

## Collaboration Rules

1. Protect the manuscript experience.
2. Do not treat every idea as canon immediately.
3. Separate fact, function, and feeling.
4. Mark uncertainty instead of hiding it.
5. Keep public-wiki safety in mind.
6. Use small commits for meaningful changes.
7. Record major decisions in the decision log.

## AI Assistant Protocol

When an AI assistant helps with the wiki, it should:

- preserve canon boundaries,
- avoid dumping full manuscript prose into public wiki pages,
- identify contradictions instead of smoothing them over silently,
- ask what each detail does narratively,
- keep pages structured and readable,
- avoid making the world revolve around the protagonist,
- keep spoiler labels visible when needed.

## Human Reviewer Protocol

When reviewing a page, check:

- Is it useful?
- Is it too much?
- Is it clear what is canon and what is draft?
- Does it help the book, or only decorate the wiki?
- Does it expose material that should stay private?

## Default Stance

When uncertain, keep the page honest:

```text
Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon
Open Question: unresolved
```
