# Open Questions

Status: Working Board
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

This page keeps uncertainty visible instead of hiding it inside scattered notes.

## How to Use This Page

Every open question should eventually move into one of three states:

- answered and promoted into canon,
- rejected and archived,
- split into smaller questions.

## Active Questions

| Question | Why It Matters | Current Direction |
|---|---|---|
| What exactly belongs in `0.md` for Book 1? | Determines whether the manuscript begins with front matter, prologue, or direct chapter prose. | Keep flexible until Chapter 1 shape stabilizes. |
| How much Star Piece logic should the wiki expose publicly? | The repository is public, so hidden canon must be handled carefully. | Keep deep mechanics restrained unless needed for development. |
| How should Vahllalhag houses appear in the opening? | Houses help structure culture, but too much exposition can slow the chapter. | Show through action first, explain later. |
| What should Muyu's first appearance reveal? | Her entrance must feel like rescue/displacement, not lore delivery. | Action before explanation. |
| How do we make Pritana feel ancient without becoming a lecture? | Historical texture must serve story rhythm. | Embed culture in tasks, rituals, food, terrain, and conflict. |
