## Review Pulse

Before promoting a major change, ask:

- Does this make the story clearer or just bigger?
- Does this create pressure for a character?
- Does this preserve the reader's wonder?
- Does this contradict the timeline?
- Does this belong in the wiki, or in the manuscript?
