# Vahllalhag

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Vahllalhag is the opening institution and settlement environment of HUMANE.

It is not only a school. It is a survival engine.

## Narrative Function

Vahllalhag lets the story introduce children, adults, ritual, training, pressure, hierarchy, and community before the cosmic rupture.

## What It Preserves

- practical skills,
- cultural memory,
- history,
- hunting,
- foraging,
- navigation,
- crafting,
- ritual continuity.

## Opening Use

The opening should make Vahllalhag feel alive through action, not exposition.
