# World Overview

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

The world of HUMANE should feel like it exists before, around, and beyond the protagonists.

## Core World Principle

The world does not organize itself around Erlen.

He is one being moving through a living Bronze Age crisis shaped by weather, trade, hunger, culture, memory, migration, and political pressure.

## Current Opening Region

The story currently opens in **Pritana**, in the southern British Isles, around **1159 B.C.E.**
