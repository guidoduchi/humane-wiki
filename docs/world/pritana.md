# Pritana

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Pritana is the cultural sphere where HUMANE currently opens.

## Narrative Function

Pritana grounds the story in land, ritual, food, family pressure, and communal survival before the Star Piece expands the scale.

## Core Texture

Pritana should be felt through:

- ash,
- resin,
- tattoos and temporary markings,
- seasonal work,
- stone and sky,
- food storage,
- tired adults,
- children learning survival as culture.
