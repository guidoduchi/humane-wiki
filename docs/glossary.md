# Glossary

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

This page collects recurring HUMANE terms for quick reference.

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| HUMANE | The novel and story universe. |
| Pritana | Cultural sphere where the story currently opens. |
| Vahllalhag | Opening institution and settlement environment. |
| Star Piece | Small engineered object that creates the central rupture. |
| Hyper Ability | A human trait forced into an extreme, costly form. |
| Mel Tor | High ground above Sharrah Pool. |
| Sharrah Pool | Pool where the Star Piece sinks and Erlen first contacts it. |

## Naming Rule

Names should be consistent across the wiki before they are treated as stable canon.
