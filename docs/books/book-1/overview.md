# Book 1 Overview

Status: Draft
Spoiler Level: Full Canon

Book 1 begins in Pritana around **1159 B.C.E.** during the wider Bronze Age collapse.

## Opening Function

The opening should establish:

- a grounded Bronze Age world,
- children and adults under ordinary pressure,
- Vahllalhag as a living institution,
- the journey toward Mel Tor and Sharrah Pool,
- the Star Piece entering as rupture, not exposition.

## Manuscript Boundary

This wiki tracks what Book 1 is supposed to do.

The actual prose belongs in the manuscript repository.
